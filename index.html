Our Heritage

Crafting Sweet
Memories

Founded in 2024, Cake & Bake began with a simple philosophy: that every celebration deserves a masterpiece. Our journey started in a small kitchen with a passion for blending traditional French techniques with modern aesthetics.

We believe that the texture of the crumb is just as important as the elegance of the finish. Using only the finest ingredients—Madagascan vanilla, Belgian chocolate, and locally sourced fruits—we create confections that are as delightful to the palate as they are to the eye.
